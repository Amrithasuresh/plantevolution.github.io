Projects


Major Researches

1. Innate Immunity
Deciphering Toll-like receptor signaling systems and controlling networks in immune cells.

2. Systems Biology
Understanding the interactions of functioning components using network perturbations and the measurement of cellular responses.

3. Network Analysis
Identification of the signal networks using genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics, methylation, RNAi, etc.

4. Stem Cells
Induced pluripotent stem cell lines derived from human somatic cells

Research Field Keywords
Innate immunity, Toll-like receptor, Systems immunology, NF-kB, IkB, ATF3, TRAF6, DNA microarray, Gene expression, RNAi, G protein Ga13, Induced pluripotent stem cells
